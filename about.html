About AUTO-LEDs

Leading the way in automotive lighting innovation since 2015

Our Story

AUTO-LEDs was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to revolutionize automotive lighting through cutting-edge LED technology. Our journey began when our founder, an automotive engineer with over 15 years of experience, noticed the limitations of traditional halogen and HID headlights.

Frustrated by the poor performance and short lifespan of existing options, he assembled a team of engineers and lighting specialists to develop a new generation of LED headlights that would outperform anything on the market.

Today, we serve customers in over 30 countries, providing high-performance LED lighting solutions for all types of vehicles - from compact cars to heavy-duty trucks. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in the automotive lighting industry.
