Vehicle Compatibility Guide

Important Notes

  • Universal Compatibility: Some models (such as H4, H11) are labeled "Universal", which means they are compatible with most vehicles with the same interface. It is recommended to confirm the original bulb interface model of your vehicle before installation.
  • Motorcycle Compatibility: Only the H4 model is explicitly designed for motorcycles (Haochen T1-H4 specific design). Other models need to be evaluated based on the motorcycle's interface specifications.
  • Year Range: The years in the table represent the mainstream range mentioned in search results and catalogs. Please refer to your vehicle's factory configuration for specific compatibility.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know which LED headlight model fits my vehicle?
Refer to our compatibility table above, or check your vehicle's owner manual for the bulb type/socket specification. You can also contact our support team with your vehicle make, model, and year for specific recommendations.
