Installation Guide

Easy plug-and-play installation. The L5 Series LED headlights feature a straightforward installation process with no modifications required to your vehicle's electrical system.

Step-by-Step Installation

  1. Disconnect the vehicle battery for safety
  2. Remove the old headlight bulbs from their sockets
  3. Install the L5 Series LED bulbs - ensure proper alignment
  4. Connect the built-in smart chip system
  5. Ensure the dual copper tube heat dissipation system has adequate clearance
  6. Adjust the beam pattern for optimal road coverage

Installation Tips

  • Ensure the vehicle is turned off before starting
  • Handle bulbs with clean hands or gloves
  • Check beam alignment after installation
  • Ensure adequate airflow around the heat dissipation system
