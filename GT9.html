Installation Guide

The GT9 Series features professional-grade installation with advanced cooling system integration for optimal performance.

Step-by-Step Installation

  1. Disconnect the vehicle battery for safety
  2. Remove the old headlight bulbs from their sockets
  3. Install the GT9 Series LED bulbs - ensure proper alignment
  4. Position the cooling fan for optimal airflow and clearance
  5. Secure all connections and ensure no obstructions to the fan
  6. Adjust the beam pattern for optimal road coverage
  7. Reconnect the battery and test the lights

Cooling System Note

The GT9 Series features a powerful 28000 RPM DC fan cooling system. Ensure adequate clearance around the fan for proper airflow and avoid contact with other engine components for optimal heat dissipation and performance.
