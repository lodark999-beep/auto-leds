L3 Series LED Headlight

40W / 4000LM • Intelligent Temperature Control

Intelligent Temperature Control System

The L3 Series features an advanced thermal management system that automatically adjusts to maintain optimal operating temperature. This intelligent technology ensures consistent performance and extends the LED lifespan by preventing overheating, even during extended use in demanding conditions.

Stable Voltage Operation

Engineered for consistent performance across varying voltage conditions (9-60V), the L3 Series eliminates flickering and ensures reliable operation regardless of your vehicle's electrical system fluctuations. This voltage stability guarantees smooth, uninterrupted illumination in all driving scenarios.

Wide Temperature Range Operation

Built to withstand extreme environmental conditions, the L3 Series operates reliably from -40°C to 80°C. This wide temperature range makes it suitable for all climates and seasons, ensuring dependable performance whether you're driving in freezing winter conditions or hot summer temperatures.

Energy Efficient Performance

With only 40W power consumption delivering 4000LM brightness, the L3 Series offers excellent energy efficiency. This optimal power-to-light ratio provides significant illumination improvement over standard halogen bulbs while maintaining low energy consumption, making it an eco-friendly and cost-effective lighting solution.

Installation Guide

Easy plug-and-play installation. Simply remove your old halogen bulbs and replace them with the L3 Series LED headlights.

Step-by-Step Installation

  1. Disconnect the vehicle battery
  2. Remove the old headlight bulbs
  3. Install the L3 Series LED bulbs
  4. Ensure proper connection for intelligent temperature control
  5. Adjust the beam pattern if necessary
  6. Reconnect the battery and test the lights

Installation Tips

  • Ensure the vehicle is turned off before starting
  • Handle bulbs with clean hands or gloves
  • Check beam alignment after installation
  • The intelligent temperature control works automatically
